Welcome to the Faculty of Computing

Overview

Faculty of Computing is formerly known as the Faculty of Computer Systems & Software Engineering. It was established on 16 February 2002 to produce knowledgeable, highly skilled, and competitive graduates in the fields of software engineering, systems, and computer networks. At the beginning, the faculty offered two fields, which were Software Engineering and Networking. The faculty has also been actively involved in research and development in areas such as information systems, software engineering, computer systems, communication systems, graphics and multimedia technology, and cybersecurity to produce technologies that are relevant to industry needs.

Programs Offered

  • Bachelor of Computer Science (Software Engineering) with Honours (BCS)
  • Bachelor of Computer Science (Computer Systems & Networking) with Honours (BCN)
  • Bachelor of Computer Science (Graphics & Multimedia Technology) with Honours (BCG)
